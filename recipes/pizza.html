Buffalo Chicken Pizza

Description

Buffalo Chicken Pizza is a favorite of mine, and a great meal to eat whenever you have the time during the week. This recipe has the best balance of buffalo sauce, cool and blue cheese, and even a few add-ons like red onions, banana peppers and green onions.

Ingredients

  • Green Onions
  • Banana Peppers
  • Mozzarella
  • Buffalo Sauce
  • Blue Cheese
  • Red Onions
  • Pizza Dough
  • Pizza Sauce
  • Butter
  • Parsley
  • Pepper Flakes
  • Parmesan
  • Shredded Chicken

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 450Â° F. Note: If you have a pizza stone, put it in the oven while it preheats for more even cooking and a browner, crisper crust.
  2. Combine the shredded chicken, buffalo sauce, and 1 tablespoon of blue cheese. Let the chicken marinate while you prepare the remaining ingredients.
  3. Spread the dough onto a lightly greased, 12-inch pizza pan (or stone). holes in it with a fork to prevent the crust from bubbling up in the oven.
  4. Top with pizza sauce, then with most of the mozzarella. Add the chicken, onions, and banana peppers, then the remaining mozzarella.
  5. Drizzle with 3 tablespoons of blue cheese, along with 1-2 tablespoons of buffalo sauce.
  6. Bake for 13 minutes and remove from the oven.
  7. Combine the melted butter, Parmesan cheese, and garlic powder. Brush it over the crust of the pizza and return it to the oven.
  8. Bake for 2-3 more minutes, or until the crust begins to brown.
  9. Garnish with green onions, red pepper flakes, and parsley. Slice and serve!
